The Motobecane Jubilee Sport was a mid-level racing/road bicycle from the '80s. The frame was constructed using Columbus CrMo steel tubing. 
<img_2>
The bike came equipped with primarily Japanese components, such as Suntour derailleurs and cranks. French wheels were used for this model and Weinman brakes
<img_3>
Overall, this bike was a fairly robust bicycle. However, the frame was too large for me, so I sold it to my roommate at the time for $280. Unfortunately, I cannot remember how much I bought this bike for...
